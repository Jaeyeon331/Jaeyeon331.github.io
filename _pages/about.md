# About me
I'm a Ph.D. student in the Big Data Lab at Inha university, advised by Prof. Dong-Wan Choi.
I received my B.S. and M.S. degrees from Inha University in 2023 and 2025, respectively.

My research focuses on developing efficient AI models for real-world deployment, particularly by accelerating the inference time of Vision-Language Models, with the goal of brining AI closer to practical everyday use.
